B.Voc

(Management & Entrepreneurship)

B.Voc

(Information Technology)

If you want to build a decent career in IT sector, this training program will be of help to you. Since it is a vocational training program, it lays much stress on practical knowledge and learning.

B.Voc

(Banking & Finance)

Currently one of the most growing industry is Banking, student develops an expertise in financial sector by learning about all possible financial services and gaining experience for the same.

B.Voc

(Design Fundamentals)

The fundamentals of design are the foundation of every visual medium, from fine art to modern web design. In this course you cover the fundamentals of design applicable to interior, Fashion and Graphics Designing.

B.Voc

(Production Technology)

The curriculum of the B.Voc in Production Technology is designed to provide a strong foundation in the core principles of production engineering and technology, including manufacturing processes, production planning and control, quality control, and supply chain management. Students will also gain knowledge in areas such as product design, materials science, and lean manufacturing principles.

B.Voc

(Organic Farming)

It is a technique, which involves the cultivation of plants and rearing of animals in natural way to maintain soil fertility and ecological balance thereby minimizing pollution and wastage.

B.Voc

(Food Processing)

Food processing is the transformation of agriculture products into food. Be it manufacturing food products or its distribution, students learns every aspect of this industry.

B.Voc

(Media, Journalism & Mass Communication)

The study of the constituents of mass media such as radio, newspapers, magazines, cinema films, television, etc. which is used to convey the information to the audience is covered in this course.
